Lamborghini Sian

The Lamborghini Sian is a limited-edition hybrid supercar featuring a naturally aspirated V12 engine paired with a mild-hybrid electric motor, delivering a combined output of 819 horsepower. It showcases Lamborghini's cutting-edge design and incorporates advanced technologies, including supercapacitors for rapid energy storage and enhanced performance.

McLaren P1

The McLaren P1 is a limited-production hybrid hypercar known for its blend of high-performance gasoline-electric powertrain, producing over 900 horsepower, exceptional aerodynamics, and active suspension. It's recognized for its pioneering use of Formula 1 technology in a road car, emphasizing track performance while maintaining road usability and luxury.
